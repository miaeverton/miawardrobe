## OUTFIT CRITIQUES

### Critique: Outfit #3 from "Same Jeans Three Ways" (light wash jeans + ruffled peplum + kitten heel)

**What was wrong:**
- The ruffled peplum top has flutter sleeves, gathered square neckline, and a peplum hem — three competing details simultaneously
- The peplum's hip flare merges visually with the wide-leg jean hem — waist definition disappears
- Violates the one-volume rule (P1): peplum adds hip volume on top of the wide-leg trouser volume
- The shoe (kitten heel) was correct but cannot elevate an unresolved outfit above it
- The black pouch clutch (unstructured, soft) amplified the already-too-busy top

**Principles violated:** P1 (one volume), P3 (crop/fitted top with full bottom), body consideration (highlight waist), AB (shoe can only elevate a resolved outfit)

**What to change:**
- Keep the jeans. Keep the kitten heel. Replace the top.
- Option A: Silk tank top (TOP-008) — fitted, clean, silk register, add belt for waist
- Option B: Strapless tube top (TOP-011) — cleanest possible, kitten heel now has a resolved outfit to elevate

**Key learning:** TOP-009 (ruffled peplum) is only correct with the black/brown silk maxi skirt. It is not a jeans piece. Not a wide-leg bottom piece at all.
